About MRLIVE Project Consultants

Established over a decade ago, MRLIVE Project Consultants has grown to become one of Kerala’s most trusted names in mineral water manufacturing and water treatment consultancy. Our journey began with a simple but ambitious vision: to provide clean, safe, and pure water to everyone, whether it’s for industrial or commercial use.

We specialize in designing, commissioning, and maintaining state-of-the-art water treatment plants that meet the diverse needs of our clients. From providing solutions for potable drinking water to wastewater treatment and high-purity process water, we have the expertise to handle every aspect of water purification. Our advanced technologies, coupled with a commitment to sustainability, ensure that our clients receive the highest quality water solutions.

Our consultancy services cover a wide range of applications, including:

  • Water Treatment Chemicals
  • RO Plant Installation and Maintenance
  • Wastewater Treatment Systems
  • Filtration Solutions for Industrial and Residential Buildings
  • Soda and Soft Drink Plant Setup

At MRLIVE, our team of dedicated engineers and technicians is the backbone of our success. With more than 10 years of experience and thousands of satisfied clients across South India, we have consistently delivered projects that meet the highest engineering and safety standards. Our focus on customer satisfaction, quality, and continuous innovation has enabled us to build lasting relationships with our clients.

Whether you need consultancy for a large-scale water treatment plant or a simple filtration system, MRLIVE Project Consultants is your trusted partner for reliable and cost-effective water solutions.
